WANT TO LEARN TO PLAY SNARE/TENOR/BASS DRUM?

Wanna start a new hobby? Interested in Celtic music?
Start playing Scottish DRUMS!

Scottish pipe band drumming is arguably the most challenging and groovy of all military-related snare drumming styles. It's hip-hoppish rhythms have you playing quintruplets so groovily no-one even notices the technical subtleties.

The teacher for new drumming pupils is Manne Laukkanen. A few times a year we have some foreign teachers over for workshops. If you're interested in taking drumming lessons and later join the band, just email Manne Laukkanen: manne(at)iki.fi

The band has rehearsals once a week in Helsinki. Besides practising we also know how to have a good time!
